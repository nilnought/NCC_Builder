## Wanted photos (better than the current diagram if you ever shoot one)

Ideally Australian residential sites, ~1000 px wide, your own camera:

- A slab edge showing the 150 mm exposed edge above garden soil
- A site cut with an un-retained batter on a sloping block
- A downpipe connected to a stormwater pit / kerb discharge
- A termite durable notice in a meter box
- A silt pit with the lid off
- Surface water ponding against a slab after rain (a perfect "what goes wrong" shot)
